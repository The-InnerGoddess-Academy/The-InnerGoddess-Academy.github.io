The Inner Goddess Academy

The Inner Goddess Academy is a platform that enriches young, ambitious women with skills and information and equip them with the critical tools required to be at the forefront of innovation. Our aim is to actively increase the female workforce in India, ultimately increasing India ºs Female Labour Force Participation (FLFP). We cater to this through our specialised workshops, conferences, and consultancy programs. We hope to inculcate leadership in every woman until the glass ceiling exists no more.
