The Inspiration Workshops

A 3-hour workshop organised by TIGA to train young women in a skill from a specific domain. These workshops are facilitated by women who are considered experts in their own fields. They don't just inspire through their story but also impart their skills. These workshops are conducted once a month for 9 months in a year!
